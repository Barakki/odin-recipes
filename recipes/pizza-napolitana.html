Pizza Napolitana

Description

The Pizza Napolitana is an argentine classic, bringing freshness with it's tomato slices and sometimes a little bit of spice with garlic.

Ingredients

  • 500gr bread flour
  • 300ml water
  • 10gr salt
  • 5gr instant yeast or 25gr fresh yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Olives (unpitted are better)

Steps

  1. Making the dough

    2. To make the dough, mix the flour with the salt in a bowl.

    In another container, mix the yeast in the water until fully dissolved. Add 3 tbsp. of olive oil.

    Next, add the liquids in the bowl with the flour and start mixing with a fork or by hand. As it becomes a ball, you can throw it in the counter and start kneading it.

    After kneading it for about 5 to 10 minutes. you should have a homogeneous ball of dough. Now drizzle some olive oil in a bowl (it can be the same bowl you started with) and put in the dough. Let it rest until double the size or for about an hour.

  2. Shaping the dough

    3. Once the dough has risen, degas the dough and divide it in 2 (or 4 if you like thin crust) and reshape them into balls.

    Let them rise for at least half an hour.

    Now we'll go to the actual shaping. Start by sprinkling flour on your work surface and on the dough, then, gently start pressing the dough into a disc, you can use a rolling pin if you want. If you notice the dough stops stretching to your liking, you can let it rest for 5 minutes and then keep going.

    Sprinkle some flour on your pizza sheet and then place the dough. Let rest for 5 to 15 minutes. Now would be a good time to preheat your oven to 180-200Â°C if you haven't already.

  3. Actual cooking

    4. Before putting the pizza in the oven, stab the dough with a fork several time to avoid big bubbles.

    Now, add a layer of sauce to the pizza and put it in the oven for 10 minutes.

    While the pizza is in the oven, it is a good time to shred the cheese, slice the tomato in thin slices and mince the garlic.

    After 10 minutes, take the pizza out of the oven, add a more sauce to your liking, add the cheese, then the tomato and sprinkle the minced garlic on top.

    Get the pizza back in the oven for 5 to 10 more minutes or until the bottom is golden brown.

    After getting the pizza out of the oven, you can sprinkle some oregano and add some olives, but let the pizza rest for at least 5 minutes before slicing.

  4. Enjoy!
